Welcome to Palawan

About Palawan

Palawan City doesn’t officially exist, but most people mean Puerto Princesa City, the capital of Palawan province. It’s known for the Puerto Princesa Underground River, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the New 7 Wonders of Nature. The city is also a gateway to beautiful beaches, island-hopping tours, and eco-adventures in the rest of Palawan..

Top Attractions

sabang wharf Sabang Wharf. Sabang is a small village located on the western coast of Palawan, Philippines.

barracuda lake In Coron, Barracuda Lake is known for its diving site, where you can find bones of fish on the shoreline.

Twin Lagoon entrance Twin Lagoon is one of the most majestic spots not only in Coron but in the whole Philippines.

Events

Watch out for the Baragatan Festival, Baragatan Festival is the biggest convergence festival, a cultural event, and founding anniversary in the province of Palawan, Philippines held every June 23. Also called Baragatan sa Palawan Festival, it is a commemoration of the establishment of its first civil government after the end of the Philippine-American War.
